Why do we miss sometimes?

There are three main reasons as to why our application is not 100% accurate.

  • Many mushrooms have overlapping characteristics, making it difficult for the computer to differentiate between them
  • The labels for each image were given to us while web scraping, and therefore may not have been fully accurate.
  • There were several hundred images to train in a short time frame, resulting in inaccuracy in prediction.
